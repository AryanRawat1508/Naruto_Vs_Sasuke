Naruto Vs Sasuke

Naruto
The protaginist of the anime series called Naruto and Naruto Shippuden.
  • Can use only ninjutsu and taijutsu.
  • Special Abilities: Kurama Chakra mode , Sage Mode and Baryon Mode
  • Special Attacks: Rasengan , Wind Style: Rasenshuriken , Shadow Shuriken Technique etc.
Summonings Used :- Toad (Gamabunta and Gamakichi)
Sasuke
The deuteragonist of the anime series called Naruto and Naruto Shippuden.
  • Can use ninjutsu , taijutsu and genjutsu.
  • Special Abilities: Sharingan, Mangekyo Sharingan
  • Special Attacks: Chidori, Amateratsu, Susaano etc.
Summonings Used :- Purple Snake (Aoda) , Hawk (Garuda).
Although both the characters went through the same past as they had no love of their parents but they both chose two different paths in their lifes and became fan favourite in different ways.
